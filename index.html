The Ethiopian Community Association in Maryland

"Empowering and uplifting the Ethiopian community through cultural connection, support, and service."

Mission

The Ethiopian Community Association in Maryland (ECAM) is a not-for-profit, non-political, non-religious, tax-exempt organization registered in Maryland committed to serving Ethiopians' educational, cultural, psychological, and socio-economic needs in Maryland.

Vision

Build a thriving, empowered, and fully integrated community that can share its culture and values while taking full advantage of the socio- economic and educational opportunities available in the larger society.
